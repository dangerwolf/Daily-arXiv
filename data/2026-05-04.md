### [1] [Beyond Rows to Reasoning: Agentic Retrieval for Multimodal Spreadsheet Understanding and Editing](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06503)
*Anmol Gulati,Sahil Sen,Waqar Sarguroh,Kevin Paul*

Main category: cs.CL

TL;DR: BRTR是一个用于理解电子表格的多模态智能体框架，通过迭代式工具调用取代单遍检索，实现了从复杂分析到结构化编辑的端到端Excel工作流，并在三个基准测试中取得了最先进的性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有技术在处理包含数百万单元格、跨工作表依赖和嵌入式视觉构件的企业电子表格时，由于单遍检索、压缩导致的数据分辨率损失以及超出LLM上下文窗口的注入，无法进行可靠的多步推理。

Method: BRTR采用迭代式工具调用循环取代单遍检索，支持端到端的Excel工作流，并评估了多种多模态嵌入模型和LLM，进行了消融实验和成本分析，同时保持了完整的事后审计能力。

Result: BRTR在FRTR-Bench、SpreadsheetLLM和FINCH三个基准测试中均达到最先进水平，分别领先先前方法25、7和32个百分点。NVIDIA NeMo Retriever 1B被证明是处理混合表格和视觉数据的最佳模型，GPT-5.2在效率-准确性方面表现最佳。

Conclusion: BRTR通过其迭代式工具调用方法，克服了现有技术的局限性，在电子表格理解方面取得了显著的性能提升，并提供了完整的审计追踪能力。

Abstract: Recent advances in multimodal Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) enable Large Language Models (LLMs) to analyze enterprise spreadsheet workbooks containing millions of cells, cross-sheet dependencies, and embedded visual artifacts. However, state-of-the-art approaches exclude critical context through single-pass retrieval, lose data resolution through compression, and exceed LLM context windows through naive full-context injection, preventing reliable multi-step reasoning over complex enterprise workbooks. We introduce Beyond Rows to Reasoning (BRTR), a multimodal agentic framework for spreadsheet understanding that replaces single-pass retrieval with an iterative tool-calling loop, supporting end-to-end Excel workflows from complex analysis to structured editing. Supported by over 200 hours of expert human evaluation, BRTR achieves state-of-the-art performance across three frontier spreadsheet understanding benchmarks, surpassing prior methods by 25 percentage points on FRTR-Bench, 7 points on SpreadsheetLLM, and 32 points on FINCH. We evaluate five multimodal embedding models, identifying NVIDIA NeMo Retriever 1B as the top performer for mixed tabular and visual data, and vary nine LLMs. Ablation experiments confirm that the planner, retrieval, and iterative reasoning each contribute substantially, and cost analysis shows GPT-5.2 achieves the best efficiency-accuracy trade-off. Throughout all evaluations, BRTR maintains full auditability through explicit tool-call traces.

### [2] [Finch: Benchmarking Finance & Accounting across Spreadsheet-Centric Enterprise Workflows](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.13168)
*Haoyu Dong,Pengkun Zhang,Yan Gao,Xuanyu Dong,Yilin Cheng,Mingzhe Lu,Zikun Zhu,Adina Yakefu,Shuxin Zheng*

Main category: cs.AI

TL;DR: Finch 是一个评估金融和会计工作流中 AI 代理的基准。它使用来自真实企业环境（包括安然公司）的数据，包含 172 个复杂工作流，旨在模拟真实世界的挑战。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 评估 AI 代理在现实世界、企业级金融和会计工作流中的能力，这些工作流涉及数据输入、结构化、格式化、网络搜索、跨文件检索、计算、建模、验证、翻译、可视化和报告等多种任务。

Method: 通过结合使用大语言模型辅助挖掘和专家注释来构建工作流。具体步骤包括：1. 使用大语言模型辅助、专家验证从真实的电子邮件线索和电子表格版本历史记录中提取工作流；2. 进行细致的注释，耗时超过 700 小时。这产生了 172 个包含 384 个任务的复合工作流，涉及 1,710 个电子表格、2700 万个单元格以及 PDF 等文件。

Conclusion: 现实世界中的企业工作流对 AI 代理构成了严峻挑战，即使是像 GPT-5.1 这样先进的模型也难以达到很高的成功率。Finch 基准为进一步研究和改进 AI 代理处理复杂金融和会计任务的能力提供了宝贵的资源。

Abstract: We introduce FinWorkBench (a.k.a. Finch) for evaluating AI agents on real-world, enterprise-grade finance and accounting workflows that interleave data entry, structuring, formatting, web search, cross-file retrieval, calculation, modeling, validation, translation, visualization, and reporting. Finch is sourced from authentic enterprise workspaces from Enron (15,000 files and 500,000 emails) and other financial institutions, covering the period 2000--2025 and preserving the in-the-wild messiness of multimodal artifacts such as tables and charts across diverse domains including budgeting, trading, asset management, and operational management. We propose a workflow construction process that combines LLM-assisted mining of workflows from authentic enterprise environments with expert annotation: (1) LLM-assisted, expert-verified derivation of workflows from real-world email threads and spreadsheet version histories, and (2) meticulous annotation requiring over 700 hours of expert effort. This yields 172 composite workflows with 384 tasks, involving 1,710 spreadsheets with 27 million cells, along with PDFs and other artifacts, capturing the intrinsically messy, long-horizon, knowledge-intensive, and collaborative nature of real-world enterprise work. We conduct both human and automated evaluations of frontier AI systems, including GPT-5.1, Claude Sonnet 4.5, Claude Opus 4.5, Gemini 3 Pro, Grok 4, and Qwen 3 Max. Under human evaluation, GPT-5.1 Pro spends an average of 16.8 minutes per workflow yet passes only 38.4% of workflows. Comprehensive case studies further surface the challenges that real-world enterprise workflows pose for AI agents.

### [3] [Bridging Natural Language and Interactive What-If Interfaces via LLM-Generated Declarative Specification](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07652)
*Sneha Gathani,Sirui Zeng,Diya Patel,Ryan Rossi,Dan Marshall,Cagatay Demiralp,Steven Drucker,Zhicheng Liu*

Main category: cs.AI

TL;DR: 本研究提出了一种两阶段工作流程，利用中间表示（PSL）将自然语言（NL）的what-if分析（WIA）问题转化为交互式可视化界面，以解决当前工具在支持有效交互式WIA方面的不足。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当前工具（如电子表格和BI工具）在设置方面耗时耗力，而基于LLM的聊天机器人界面存在语义脆弱、误解用户意图和结果不一致等问题，因此需要更有效的交互式WIA支持。

Method: 该工作流程分为两步：1. 使用LLM从NL问题生成PSL规范，捕捉分析意图和逻辑，并允许对错误的规范进行验证和修复。2. 将规范编译成具有参数控件和链接可视化的交互式可视化界面。

Result: 在包含405个WIA问题的基准测试中，LLM生成的规范有52.42%无需干预即可正确生成。通过分析失败案例并应用基于错误分类的修复，成功率提高到80.42%。研究还表明，未检测到的功能错误可能导致生成误导性的界面。

Conclusion: 中间的PSL规范对于在LLM驱动的WIA系统中可靠地连接NL和交互式WIA界面至关重要，有助于避免因未检测到的错误而产生误导性的可视化结果。

Abstract: What-if analysis (WIA) is an iterative, multi-step process where users explore and compare hypothetical scenarios by adjusting parameters, applying constraints, and scoping data through interactive interfaces. Current tools fall short of supporting effective interactive WIA: spreadsheet and BI tools require time-consuming and laborious setup, while LLM-based chatbot interfaces are semantically fragile, frequently misinterpret intent, and produce inconsistent results as conversations progress. To address these limitations, we present a two-stage workflow that translates natural language (NL) WIA questions into interactive visual interfaces via an intermediate representation, powered by the Praxa Specification Language (PSL): first, LLMs generate PSL specifications from NL questions capturing analytical intent and logic, enabling validation and repair of erroneous specifications; and second, the specifications are compiled into interactive visual interfaces with parameter controls and linked visualizations. We benchmark this workflow with 405 WIA questions spanning 11 WIA types, 5 datasets, and 3 state-of-the-art LLMs. The results show that across models, half of specifications (52.42%) are generated correctly without intervention. We perform an analysis of the failure cases and derive an error taxonomy spanning non-functional errors (specifications fail to compile) and functional errors (specifications compile but misrepresent intent). Based on the taxonomy, we apply targeted repairs on the failure cases using few-shot prompts and improve the success rate to 80.42%. Finally, we show how undetected functional errors propagate through compilation into plausible but misleading interfaces, demonstrating that the intermediate specification is critical for reliably bridging NL and interactive WIA interface in LLM-powered WIA systems.

### [4] [Topo-RAG: Topology-aware retrieval for hybrid text-table documents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.10215)
*Alex Dantart,Marco Kóvacs-Navarro*

Main category: cs.AI

TL;DR: Topo-RAG框架通过处理数据的拓扑结构来改进检索，在混合查询方面比标准线性化方法提高了18.4%的nDCG@10。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当前的企业数据集检索系统（RAG）将表格等结构化数据线性化为文本，这种方法在数学上是不充分的，无法捕捉数据的多维度几何特征。

Method: Topo-RAG提出了一种双重架构，将文本信息通过传统的密集检索器处理，而表格结构则通过细胞感知后期交互机制处理，以保留其空间关系。

Result: 在模拟真实世界复杂性的SEC-25数据集上进行评估，Topo-RAG在混合查询方面比标准线性化方法在nDCG@10方面提高了18.4%。

Conclusion: Topo-RAG框架通过尊重数据的拓扑结构，超越了“一切皆文本”的假设，显著提高了对包含文本和表格混合信息的检索能力。

Abstract: In enterprise datasets, documents are rarely pure. They are not just text, nor just numbers; they are a complex amalgam of narrative and structure. Current Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems have attempted to address this complexity with a blunt tool: linearization. We convert rich, multidimensional tables into simple Markdown-style text strings, hoping that an embedding model will capture the geometry of a spreadsheet in a single vector. But it has already been shown that this is mathematically insufficient.
  This work presents Topo-RAG, a framework that challenges the assumption that "everything is text". We propose a dual architecture that respects the topology of the data: we route fluid narrative through traditional dense retrievers, while tabular structures are processed by a Cell-Aware Late Interaction mechanism, preserving their spatial relationships. Evaluated on SEC-25, a synthetic enterprise corpus that mimics real-world complexity, Topo-RAG demonstrates an 18.4% improvement in nDCG@10 on hybrid queries compared to standard linearization approaches. It's not just about searching better; it's about understanding the shape of information.

### [5] [SheetDesigner: MLLM-Powered Spreadsheet Layout Generation with Rule-Based and Vision-Based Reflection](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.07473)

Main category: cs.AI

TL;DR: 本篇论文研究了自动化电子表格布局生成问题，提出了SheetDesigner框架，并进行了实验评估。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 电子表格在数据处理中至关重要，但手动设计布局耗时耗力，因此需要自动化解决方案。现有模型忽视了电子表格离散的网格结构和独特的语义关联性。

Method: 提出SheetDesigner框架，利用多模态大语言模型（MLLMs），结合规则和视觉反射，实现了组件放置和内容填充，并进行了形式化定义和实验评估。

Result: SheetDesigner在3,326个电子表格数据集上进行了评估，表现优于五个基线模型，效果提升至少22.6%。研究发现MLLMs通过视觉模态能很好地处理重叠和平衡问题，但在对齐方面存在不足，需要结合规则和视觉反射策略。

Conclusion: SheetDesigner框架能够有效地生成电子表格布局，通过结合规则和视觉反射策略可以弥补MLLMs在某些方面的不足。

Abstract: Spreadsheets are critical to data-centric tasks, with rich, structured layouts that enable efficient information transmission. In this paper, we first formalize the spreadsheet layout generation task, supported by a seven-criterion evaluation protocol and a dataset of 3,326 spreadsheets. We then introduce SheetDesigner, a zero-shot and training-free framework using Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) that combines rule and vision reflection for component placement and content population. SheetDesigner outperforms five baselines by at least 22.6\%. We further find that through vision modality, MLLMs handle overlap and balance well but struggle with alignment, necessitates hybrid rule and visual reflection strategies. Our codes and data is available at Github.

### [6] [Leveraging Test Driven Development with Large Language Models for Reliable and Verifiable Spreadsheet Code Generation: A Research Framework](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.15585)
*Simon Thorne,Advait Sarkar*

Main category: cs.SE

TL;DR: LLM 生成的代码和电子表格逻辑存在错误，提出 TDD 框架来提高准确性和可靠性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: LLM 在生成软件代码和电子表格逻辑时存在幻觉、逻辑不一致和语法错误等问题，尤其是在金融和科学等高风险领域，准确性和可靠性至关重要。

Method: 提出一个结构化的研究框架，将测试驱动开发（TDD）与大语言模型（LLM）的生成相结合，以提高生成内容的正确性、可靠性和用户信心。该框架强调“先测试”的方法，为 LLM 提供技术约束和认知支架，以生成更准确、可验证和可理解的解决方案。框架适用于多种编程环境，并包含明确的实验设计、参与者分组、评估指标和基于 TDD 的提示示例。

Result: 该框架旨在提高计算思维、提示工程技能和用户参与度，特别有助于缺乏正式编程培训但会因逻辑错误承担严重后果的电子表格用户。

Conclusion: 通过强调测试驱动的思维，旨在提高 LLM 生成内容的准确性和可靠性，并鼓励合作以进行进一步的改进和评估，最终实现 LLM 在教育和专业开发实践中的负责任和可靠的集成。

Abstract: Large Language Models (LLMs), such as ChatGPT, are increasingly leveraged for generating both traditional software code and spreadsheet logic. Despite their impressive generative capabilities, these models frequently exhibit critical issues such as hallucinations, subtle logical inconsistencies, and syntactic errors, risks particularly acute in high stakes domains like financial modelling and scientific computations, where accuracy and reliability are paramount. This position paper proposes a structured research framework that integrates the proven software engineering practice of Test-Driven Development (TDD) with Large Language Model (LLM) driven generation to enhance the correctness of, reliability of, and user confidence in generated outputs. We hypothesise that a "test first" methodology provides both technical constraints and cognitive scaffolding, guiding LLM outputs towards more accurate, verifiable, and comprehensible solutions. Our framework, applicable across diverse programming contexts, from spreadsheet formula generation to scripting languages such as Python and strongly typed languages like Rust, includes an explicitly outlined experimental design with clearly defined participant groups, evaluation metrics, and illustrative TDD based prompting examples. By emphasising test driven thinking, we aim to improve computational thinking, prompt engineering skills, and user engagement, particularly benefiting spreadsheet users who often lack formal programming training yet face serious consequences from logical errors. We invite collaboration to refine and empirically evaluate this approach, ultimately aiming to establish responsible and reliable LLM integration in both educational and professional development practices.
